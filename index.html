Welcome to Music of Life

Music of Life is a cellular automaton inspired by Conway's Game of Life combined with a Tone Matrix. Each cell has a musical tone that is sounded when activated during the sequence.

Select a preset configuration of cells or create your own, then click 'Start' to commence the sequence and hear its naturally evolving musical phrase.

While sequence is occurring, cells evolve like so:
1) Live cells with fewer than two live neighbors die.
2) Live cells with more than three live neighbors die.
3) Live cells with two or three live neighbors survive.
4) Dead cells with three live neighbors come alive.
